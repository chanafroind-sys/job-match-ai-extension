Platform Engineer — Core Services

Join our platform team to build and operate the server-side services that
power our product.

Responsibilities:
- Build and maintain server-side services that handle high request volumes
- Own the design of our relational data store schemas and query efficiency
- Package and deploy services using container orchestration tooling
- Partner with a small, senior engineering group on system design decisions
- Drive down latency and improve reliability of core service endpoints

Requirements:
- 4+ years building server-side services in a modern scripting language
- Strong grasp of relational data stores and how to keep them performant at scale
- Experience packaging and running workloads in containers
- Comfortable owning architecture decisions within a small engineering pod
- Track record of improving the reliability and speed of production services
